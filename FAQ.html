Popular Questions

What grades are offered at Buddha Smriti Education Foundation?

Buddha Smriti Education Foundation currently offers classes from Nursery to Class 1, with plans to expand in the future.

What is the school's teaching methodology?

Our school follows a child-centric and interactive teaching approach. We emphasize experiential learning, where students learn by doing through hands-on activities, projects, and group discussions.

Is transportation available for students?

Yes, we offer a safe and reliable transportation service for students. Our buses cover several routes around the Jhapa district.

What extracurricular activities are offered?

We offer a range of extracurricular activities including sports, arts, music, dance, and yoga, as well as community service programs.

What are the admission requirements?

Parents need to submit the application form, birth certificate, and previous school records for admissions. An interview or assessment may be required for higher grades.

Does the school have a scholarship program?

Yes, we offer scholarships based on merit and financial need. Further details can be obtained from the school office.



About Us

At Sunshine Kinder House, we believe in lighting the spark of curiosity! Located in the heart of Birtamode, Jhapa, our school is a vibrant space where imagination meets learning, empowering each child to dream big and explore their passions in a nurturing community.
